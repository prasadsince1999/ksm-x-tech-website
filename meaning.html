The Meaning Behind KSM X Tech

K reflects our family identity — the roots that shaped who I am.

KSM X Tech was founded on the values of family, integrity, and growth.

S represents Satya — my father, who dedicated his life to honest public service and helping those in need. From him, I learned integrity, discipline, and the true value of money.

M represents Manga — my mother, who nurtured me with spirituality, kindness, and strength. From her, I learned grounding, compassion, and inner balance.

The “X” symbolizes transformation — where traditional values meet modern innovation.

KSM X Tech carries these principles into every product we build: honesty, resilience, purpose, and long-term vision.
